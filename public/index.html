The Story Behind the Sauce

THE SAUCE BOSS

"I didn't come here with connections, money, or a plan B. I came with hunger — and that never left."

In 2019, Gagandeep Ghuman arrived in Regina at 19 years old with $800, two bags, and a dream. He spent the next year and a half obsessing over wings — flavor chemistry, texture, heat levels, sauce combinations nobody had tried.

When Wing-O opened on Albert Street, it was Regina's first local wings eatery — with 125+ handcrafted flavors. What started as one location grew to five across Saskatchewan. Next stop: Winnipeg.

The movement keeps growing. 🌾
